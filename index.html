Mint App – Child Safety & CSAE Policy

At Mint, we are committed to protecting users from child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We take the following steps to ensure the safety and security of our community:

  • We strictly prohibit any form of CSAE content or behavior.

  • Accounts found to be violating CSAE policies are immediately suspended or banned, and reported to relevant authorities.
  • Our team reviews reported content and takes swift action based on community guidelines.
  • We do not allow users under the age of 13 to register on the app.
